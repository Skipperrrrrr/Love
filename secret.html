Just One More Secret…

When morning winds whisper your name through the curtains,
I find new reasons to stay awake in the daylight of your smile.

When sunsets spill gold on your skin,
I swear the horizon bows to watch you glow.

When night crickets sing, I count heartbeats instead of stars—
every pulse another promise of “always.”

I have searched the dictionary of the universe for
words wide enough, oceans deep enough,
futures bright enough to describe this love.

Will you marry me?
